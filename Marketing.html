MARKETING

Bill Greene Consulting, LLC develops marketing plans that are based on the mission and strategic objectives of your organization. We design marketing and public relations campaigns that elevate the community's understanding of, and support for, your organization.

Bill Greene Consulting, LLC creates events that attract, and provide a vehicle to communicate with, your organization's target audience, such as philanthropists and community leaders.

Marketing and Public Relations plans include:

  • An analysis of the present situation
  • An analysis of the market and target audiences
  • Marketing objectives
  • Strategies to meet the objectives
  • Marketing programs, tools and events
  • Metrics and a process to measure results
  • Identification of marketing expertise and resources needed to execute the campaign or event
