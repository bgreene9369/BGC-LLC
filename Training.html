TRAINING

Bill Greene Consulting offers a wide variety of training programs individually designed to meet the needs of each client. Programs focus on the awareness, learning and development of the "people" and organizational skills needed in effective workplaces. Training programs are highly experiential and interactive taking into account the needs of adults learners. All programs are also available in Spanish.

Topics include:

  • Teamwork
  • Conflict resolution
  • Communication skills
  • Intercultural communication/awareness
  • Diversity
  • Leadership
  • Supervisory skills
  • Effective meetings
  • Outcome measurement
