ORGANIZATIONAL DEVELOPMENT CONSULTING SERVICES

Board Development Planning

Facilitating staff and volunteer collaboration to develop a written Board Development Plan that includes policies, procedures and tools around board composition gap analysis; prospect identification; prospect evaluation; recruitment practices and process; new board member orientation; annual board member evaluation; and volunteer recognition. In addition to production of the written plan, these services include a facilitated gap assessment and prospecting exercise.

Business Plan Development for New Service Site or Programs

Facilitating staff and volunteer collaboration to develop a non-profit business plan focused on sustainability by assessing feasibility, market analysis, operational expenses, revenue strategies, etc.

Strategic Planning

Facilitating staff, volunteer, client and donor collaboration to create a short-term tactical plan or long-term strategic plan. These services include data gather & analysis; mission/vision statement re-assessment; SWOT analysis; goal setting; objectives development; action planning; establishing post-planning evaluation, monitoring, indicator tools; facilitating planning retreat; and may include some post-planning executive coaching services for an additional fee.

Á la Carte Strategic Planning Services:

  • Data Gathering and Organizational Assessments
  • Planning Process Infrastructure, Baseline and Benchmarking Activities
  • Goal Setting and Objectives Development
  • Action Planning Facilitation
  • Evaluation and Implementation Tools Development
  • Strategic Planning Retreat Facilitation
Employment Search Services

In the wake of losing an employee, organizational staff and board volunteers oftentimes feel overwhelmed trying to cover the vacancy in addition to searching for a replacement. Employment Search Services include assisting staff and key HR volunteers with building qualified applicant pools, prospect screening, framing interview process, building interview question packets, facilitation and coordination of interviews and facilitation of the candidate selection decision.

Evaluation Tools and Services

Before embarking on another campaign, event or planning initiative, it is always a good idea to know where you’re at. After completing a planning initiative, it is wise to develop tools and indicators to monitor implementation progress and create a sense of accountability. The Healthy Non-Profit can help with evaluation (e.g. critique meetings and data analysis) or development of tools (e.g. scorecards, dashboards, etc). Pricing is dependent on the size of your project. So, give us a call and we’ll work on a customized proposal to meet your needs.
